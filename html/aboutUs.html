About Us



Welcome to Spice Magic By Kesar, a website dedicated to share my passion for cooking with you all. My name is Kesar Thakkar and I am a 12th grader with love for creating delicious meals using variety of spices and ingredients.

I created this website to share my recipes with others who share the same passion for cooking or are looking to learn more about it.

Spice Magic By Kesar offers a wide variety of dishes that are easy to cook. I believe that cooking is not just about making food, but also about creativity, experimentation and sharing joy with others. So come, join me on this culinary adventure and let's explore the magic of spices together!
