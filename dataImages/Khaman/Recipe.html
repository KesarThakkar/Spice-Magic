Khaman

Khaman is a savory steamed cake made with gram flour (besan), spices, and tempered with mustard seeds and curry leaves. It is a popular snack from the Indian state of Gujarat.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup gram flour (besan)
  • 1/4 cup semolina (sooji)
  • 1/4 cup yogurt (dahi)
  • 1 tsp ginger paste
  • 1 tsp green chili paste
